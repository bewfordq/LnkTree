Bewf Bot Open Beta

Free public alpha · UK only

A Vinted-to-CeX flip bot that posts possible resale opportunities into a public Telegram channel. Users can review older flips or wait for fresh alerts, then decide whether to buy instantly, lowball the seller, cash out through CeX, or resell the item themselves.

What the bot does

  • Scans Vinted listings and compares them against CeX pricing to find possible flips.
  • Posts the listing and CeX reference into the public Telegram so you can check the match yourself.
  • You can lowball the Vinted seller for more profit, or buy at the current price if the flip is still good.
  • Fastest route is taking the item to CeX for cash, but you can also self-sell it if you want to chase more profit.

It is a free alpha for review and feedback right now, not me selling the bot files.

Important: I am not responsible if you buy an item and it is different than the one the bot pairs it with. It is your responsibility to check it if you're buying something.

Self-sell upside is only a rough guide. It can use CeX retail as a fallback and does not fully account for shipping, marketplace fees, or other resale costs if you sell the item yourself.
